Frequently Asked Question

We want you to feel confident when trading – starting with understanding everything clearly.

01

How do I create a wallet?

Click the "Sign Up" button and follow the instructions to set up your wallet. You'll need a valid email and a secure password. Your wallet will be created instantly and you can start managing your crypto assets.

02

Is my crypto safe on UKX?

Your assets are protected by industry-standard security practices. For maximum safety, always use strong passwords and never share your credentials. We do not store private keys in-browser for MVP security.

03

How do I send and receive crypto?

Go to your wallet dashboard. To send, enter the recipient's address and amount, then confirm. To receive, click "Receive" to view your wallet address and QR code.

04

What tokens can I manage?

You can manage major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT. More tokens will be added as the platform grows.
